# ⚡ Bolt.score | MHTCET Analytical Pro

**Bolt.score** is a minimalist, high-speed web tool designed for MHTCET aspirants to calculate their scores immediately after the official response sheets are released. No more manual counting—just upload and get your results in a "Bolt" flash.

## 🚀 Features
- **Superfast Analysis:** Extracts data from PDF response sheets within seconds.
- **Official Logic:** Calculates marks based on official CET Cell marking schemes (Maths: 2 marks/q, Physics/Chemistry/Biology: 1 mark/q).
- **Comprehensive Scorecard:** Generates a detailed breakdown of correct/wrong answers per subject.
- **Visual Analytics:** Includes dynamic charts (Bar & Doughnut) for performance visualization.
- **Export Options:** Download your professional scorecard as a high-quality **PDF** or **Image**.
- **Privacy First:** All processing happens in the browser. Your data never leaves your device.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **HTML5 / CSS3** (Modern, responsive UI)
- **JavaScript (ES6+)**
- **PDF.js** (For high-speed text extraction)
- **Chart.js** (For performance visualization)
- **html2canvas & jsPDF** (For generating reports)

## 📖 How to Use?
1. Enter your Name, Seat Number, and Exam Details.
2. Click on **⚡ ANALYZE RESPONSE SHEET**.
3. Select your official MHTCET Response Sheet (PDF).
4. View your aggregate score and subject-wise breakdown instantly.
5. Download your report as a PDF or Image for future reference.

## ⚖️ Disclaimer
*This tool is an unofficial analyzer based on provided response sheet data. Results should be treated as estimates. For official admissions, please refer to the normalized scores provided by the CET Cell.*
